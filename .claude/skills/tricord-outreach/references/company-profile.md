## The Three-Lane AI Governance Model
Tricord's proprietary framework. Separates AI by risk profile:
- **Lane 1 — Client-Confidential AI**: Touches regulated/confidential data. Highest scrutiny.
- **Lane 2 — Business AI**: Operations, admin, productivity (Copilot, Power Automate).
- **Lane 3 — Infrastructure AI**: IT automation, security, monitoring. Managed by Tricord.
This prevents one team's productivity tool from becoming another team's compliance incident.
